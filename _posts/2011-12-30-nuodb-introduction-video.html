NuoDB is new (on Beta phase) persistent storage solution that combines:
  • Relational model (which is emulated on top of key-value storage)
  • Support of ACID transactions
  • Horizontal scalability of NoSQL solutions
According to video performance is at least 3x higher that MySQL.
